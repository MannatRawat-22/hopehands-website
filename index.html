About us

Who we are

Founded in 2015, HopeHands works with local communities to build lasting solutions — not short-term aid. We believe every person deserves access to clean water, quality education, and nutritious food regardless of where they are born.

👁

Our vision

A world where no child goes to bed hungry or grows up without access to education.

🎯

Our mission

To deliver community-led programmes that create sustainable change from the ground up.

🛡

Our values

Transparency, dignity, and community ownership in everything we do.

Our work

Causes we support

We focus on three core areas where sustained investment creates the greatest long-term impact.

💧

Clean water

Installing wells and water purification systems in 38 villages. Over 8,000 people now have safe drinking water.

🏫

Education

Running 12 community learning centres providing free education to 2,100+ children every year.

🍎

Food security

Distributing nutritious meals and training families in sustainable farming and kitchen gardening.
